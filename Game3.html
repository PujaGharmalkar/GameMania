The HangMan Game v 1.0

The Hangman v 1.0

Players: 2

Instructions:
P1: Input the secret guess word into the textbox and press 'Start game' button
P2: Try to guess the secret word by tapping or clicking on a letter or use the PC keyboard. You have 6 lives. If the word contains the letter, it will be uncovered; otherwise you lost a life
If you guess the word, you win. If you lost all of the tries, you lost. All suggestions are welcome
